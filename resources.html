Dosing and Regimen

Start Your Journey Right

The journey we have created for your 3 Completement Formulas™ remains in the spirit of Dr Carolyn Dean MD ND's vision of a simple and elegant collection of nutrients that everyone can understand. We have included 3 of our formulations that complement each other in their actions and effects both inside, and outside the body. For your best experience, make sure you are following these steps:

Start with your Vitamin C ReSet® Powder

1. Stir one scoop of Vitamin C ReSet® powder into 8-16 oz of water. This "flavor-masking" form of nutrient can be further adjusted to taste by varying your amount of water.

Combine With Your ReMag® Magnesium

2. Add a 1/4 tsp of ReMag® into your Vitamin C ReSet® water and sip throughout the day. Liquid minerals are concentrated, and it is best to spread your intake out over a period of time to for a balanced dose.

Add D3K2 ReSet® to your Regimen

3. Take one capsule of D3K2 ReSet a day. Vitamin D should never be taken without magnesium because of its requirement for activation of vitamin D into an active form. Get the most out of your vitamin D supplementation..
